Product Description:

Experience the delightful caramelized flavor of Brown Cham Cham, a unique variation of the classic Bengali sweet. Made from fresh chhena (cottage cheese) and infused with rich caramel syrup, this dessert offers a deeper, more indulgent taste. Garnished with coconut flakes or a touch of khoya, Brown Cham Cham is perfect for festive occasions, gifting, or satisfying your sweet cravings.

Ingredients: Chhena (Cottage Cheese), Caramelized Sugar Syrup, Coconut Flakes, Khoya (optional), Ghee

Shelf Life: 7-10 days when stored in a cool, dry place.

Why You’ll Love It:

  • SSoft, spongy texture with a rich caramel flavor
  • Garnished with coconut for a delightful finish
  • Perfect for celebrations, festivals, and gifting
