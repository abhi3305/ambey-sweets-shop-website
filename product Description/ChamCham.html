Product Description:

Relish the soft and juicy texture of Cham Cham, a traditional Bengali sweet made from fresh chhena (cottage cheese) and soaked in delicately flavored sugar syrup. Often garnished with coconut flakes or stuffed with a rich khoya filling, this dessert is a burst of sweetness in every bite. Perfect for celebrations, gifting, or indulging in a special treat, Cham Cham brings joy to every occasion.

Ingredients: Chhena (Cottage Cheese), Sugar, Water, Coconut Flakes, Khoya (optional), Rose Water

Shelf Life: 7-10 days when stored in a cool, dry place.

Why You’ll Love It:

  • Soft, spongy texture with a juicy, sweet flavor
  • Enhanced with coconut garnish for added appeal
  • Perfect for festive occasions, parties, and gifting
