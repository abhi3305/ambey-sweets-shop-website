Product Description:

Relish the rich, festive flavor of Gujiya, a traditional Indian sweet dumpling filled with a luscious mixture of khoya (mawa), dried fruits, and a touch of cardamom. Wrapped in a crispy, golden pastry shell and deep-fried to perfection, Gujiya is a must-have for celebrations like Holi, Diwali, or any special occasion.

Ingredients: Refined Flour, Khoya (Mawa), Sugar, Almonds, Cashews, Raisins, Cardamom, Ghee

Shelf Life: 7-10 days when stored in an airtight container in a cool, dry place.
