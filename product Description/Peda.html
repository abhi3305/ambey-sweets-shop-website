Product Description:

Savor the classic taste of Peda, a traditional Indian sweet made with khoya (reduced milk), sugar, and a hint of cardamom. These soft and creamy treats are delicately shaped and often adorned with a touch of saffron or pistachios for an elegant finish. Perfect for festivals, celebrations, or gifting, Peda is a timeless delicacy loved by all.

Ingredients: Khoya (Reduced Milk), Sugar, Cardamom, Ghee, Saffron, Pistachios

Shelf Life: 10-15 days when stored in a cool, dry place.

Why You’ll Love It:

  • Rich, creamy flavor with a soft, melt-in-your-mouth texture
  • Made with traditional recipes and high-quality ingredients
  • Ideal for festive gifting and personal indulgence
