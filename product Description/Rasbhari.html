Product Description:

Delight in the juicy sweetness of Rasbhari, a traditional Indian dessert crafted from soft, spongy balls made with fresh chhena (cottage cheese) and soaked in a lightly flavored sugar syrup. Its golden color and melt-in-your-mouth texture make it a perfect treat for festive occasions, celebrations, or simply indulging in something special.

Ingredients: Chhena (Cottage Cheese), Sugar, Water, Rose Essence (optional)

Shelf Life: 7-10 days when stored in a cool, dry place.

Why You’ll Love It:

  • Soft and juicy with a light, sweet flavor
  • Made using traditional recipes for an authentic taste
  • Perfect for gifting, festivals, and family celebrations
