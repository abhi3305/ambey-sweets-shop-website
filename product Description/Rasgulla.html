Product Description:

Dive into the sweetness of Rasgulla, a classic Bengali dessert made from soft, spongy balls of chhena (cottage cheese) soaked in light sugar syrup. Known for its delicate texture and juicy sweetness, Rasgulla is a refreshing and indulgent treat for all occasions, from festive celebrations to everyday dessert cravings.

Ingredients: Chhena (Cottage Cheese), Sugar, Water, Rose Water (optional)

Shelf Life: 7-10 days when stored in a cool, dry place.

Why You’ll Love It:

  • Soft, spongy texture soaked in sweet syrup
  • Made with fresh ingredients and traditional techniques
  • Perfect for gifting, festive occasions, or a light dessert
