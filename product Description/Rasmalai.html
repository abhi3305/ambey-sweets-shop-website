Product Description:

Indulge in the luxurious taste of Rasmalai, a beloved Indian dessert made with soft, spongy cottage cheese patties soaked in a rich, saffron-infused milk sauce. Flavored with cardamom and garnished with slivers of almonds and pistachios, each bite offers a delightful balance of creaminess and sweetness, perfect for celebrations or special moments.

Ingredients: Cottage Cheese (Chenna), Milk, Sugar, Saffron, Cardamom, Almonds, Pistachios

Shelf Life: Best enjoyed fresh; can be refrigerated for up to 2-3 days.

Why You’ll Love It:

  • Soft, creamy texture with a hint of saffron and cardamom
  • Made with fresh milk and premium ingredients
  • Perfect for festive occasions and special treats
