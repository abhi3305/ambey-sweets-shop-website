Product Description:

Experience the light and flaky texture of Soan Papdi, a classic Indian dessert known for its unique melt-in-your-mouth consistency. Made from gram flour, ghee, and sugar, and delicately flavored with cardamom, this sweet treat is garnished with almonds and pistachios for an irresistible finish. Perfect for festivals, gifting, or indulging your sweet tooth.

Ingredients: Gram Flour (Besan), Sugar, Ghee, Cardamom, Almonds, Pistachios

Shelf Life: 20-25 days when stored in an airtight container in a cool, dry place.
