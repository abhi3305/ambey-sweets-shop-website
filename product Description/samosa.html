Product Description:

Enjoy the classic taste of Samosa, a popular Indian snack with a crispy, golden pastry shell filled with a deliciously spiced potato and pea mixture. Each bite offers a burst of flavors, thanks to aromatic spices and herbs, making it the perfect snack for any occasion—be it tea time, parties, or a quick bite on the go.
